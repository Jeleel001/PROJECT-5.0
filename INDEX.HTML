Tom Yum Goon:A spicy Thai Delight

Tom Yum Goong is a classic Thai soup known for its bold flavors and aromatic spices.This hot and sour soup features a tantalizing blend of lemongrass,galangal,and of kaffir lime leaves,combined with succulent shrimp

Ingredients:

  • Lemongrass:2 stalks
  • basil:1 cup
  • Lime Leaves:3 leaves
  • Shrimp:500g

Preparation:

  1. Simmer the broth for 10 minuites.
  2. Add,galangal,and kaffir lime leaves
  3. Stir in shrimp and cook until pink
  4. Season with fish sauce ,limejuice and chill paste
  5. Garnish with Thai basil and serve hot

Cooking Tips

  • 1/4 cup of fish sauce adds authentic Thai flavor
  • 1 teaspoon of chili paste gives the soup its signature heat


Ingredients Quantity
Lemongrass 2 Stalk
Thai basil 1 cup
Kaffir Lime 3 Leaves
